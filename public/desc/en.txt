This mod revamps the battle queue screen:
- The tank icon is upscaled to 3x
- The rest of the elements is moved to the right side
- The selected game mode's corresponding keyart is displayed as background
- Other tweaks (display background for the back button, vertically shrink area, occupied by the controls, etc)

Compatibility:
- Platform: any
- Game publisher: any
- Game version: 5.6.0+
